About Hair Affair

40+ Years in Business
10 Years Current Ownership

Hair Affair has been a cornerstone of the community for over four decades, providing exceptional hair care services with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Under current ownership for the past 10 years, we continue to uphold the tradition of excellence while embracing modern techniques and trends.

Our experienced team of stylists is dedicated to helping you achieve your perfect look, whether you're seeking a subtle refresh or a dramatic transformation. We believe that great hair is the best accessory, and we're here to help you wear it with confidence.
